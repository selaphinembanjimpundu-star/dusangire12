About Dusangire

Our Mission

To bridge critical health gaps by delivering medically-approved, convenient nutrition directly to patients and medical staff within hospitals, accelerating recovery and improving health outcomes.

Our Vision

A national network of hospital-integrated nutrition centers across Rwanda, where every patient has access to personalized, healing meals that support their recovery journey.

Our Values

Quality: Every meal meets medical standards. Compassion: We care about recovery. Innovation: Technology serving health.

Who We Are

Dusangire, meaning "Let's share" in Kinyarwanda, is a specialized hospital restaurant service that integrates nutritional expertise with hospital operations. We focus on the critical gap in hospital care: inadequate access to diet-specific meals for optimal patient recovery.

Our team includes certified nutritionists, experienced chefs, and technology experts dedicated to making healthy nutrition accessible, convenient, and integrated into the hospital experience.

What We Deliver

  • Medically-Tailored Menus - Designed for specific health conditions
  • Bedside Convenience - Order from your room anytime
  • Quality Assurance - Clinical nutritionist oversight
  • Local Impact - Supporting Rwandan farmers and jobs
