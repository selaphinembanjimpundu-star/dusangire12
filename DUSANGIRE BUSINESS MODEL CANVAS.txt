DUSANGIRE - BUSINESS MODEL CANVAS
Hospital E-Commerce Restaurant & Nutritionist Platform
================================================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

DUSANGIRE is an innovative hospital-based e-commerce restaurant system that 
addresses critical malnutrition challenges in healthcare settings through:
- Technology-enabled meal ordering and delivery
- Nutritionist-guided meal plans
- Convenient bedside ordering system
- Medically-approved, health-focused meals

TARGET MARKET OPPORTUNITY
- Rwanda: 12+ million patients admitted annually for medical reasons
- 70% are inpatients requiring continuous nutrition management
- 47.3% are malnourished at admission
- Malnutrition increases to 60% after one week of hospitalization
- This trend is consistent across acute care settings globally

================================================================================
PROBLEM STATEMENTS (Market Need)
================================================================================

1. IRREGULAR EATING SCHEDULE & UNBALANCED DIET
   → Linked to higher rates of complications, decreased immunity, poor wound
     healing, increased infection rates, and higher mortality

2. LACK OF NUTRITIONAL KNOWLEDGE
   → Patients and caregivers lack understanding of dietary requirements
   → No personalized nutrition guidance available

3. POOR HYGIENE STANDARDS
   → Inconsistent food safety and sanitation practices
   → Risk of foodborne illnesses in vulnerable populations

4. MISALIGNMENT BETWEEN PATIENT NEEDS & CAREGIVERS
   → Disconnect between healthcare provider recommendations and actual meals
   → No mechanism to ensure prescribed diets are followed

5. CAREGIVER TIME CONSTRAINTS
   → Abagemura (traditional caregivers) lack sufficient time for feeding
     assistance with patients needing help
   → Manual coordination creates bottlenecks

6. OVERCROWDING & RESOURCE STRAIN
   → Over-population of caregivers from different regions
   → Hospital becomes congested, affecting patient care quality

STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT: SOLUTIONS TO PROBLEMS
================================================================================

PROBLEM 1: Irregular Eating & Unbalanced Diet
SOLUTION: 
  • App-based meal ordering ensures consistent meal timing
  • Nutritionist-designed menus ensure balanced nutrition
  • Subscription plans guarantee daily meal provision
  • Result: Reduced complications, better immunity, faster wound healing

PROBLEM 2: Lack of Nutritional Knowledge
SOLUTION:
  • Nutritionist consultations available to all users
  • In-app health education and meal information
  • Clear ingredient lists and nutritional breakdowns
  • Personalized meal recommendations based on health profile
  • Result: Educated patients and caregivers make better choices

PROBLEM 3: Poor Hygiene Standards
SOLUTION:
  • Professional kitchen with food safety protocols
  • Certified staff trained in food handling
  • Regular health inspections and compliance
  • Packaged meals with proper sanitation
  • Result: Reduced foodborne illnesses and infections

PROBLEM 4: Misalignment Between Patient Needs & Caregivers
SOLUTION:
  • Healthcare provider meal prescriptions integrated into system
  • Real-time notifications of dietary requirements
  • Verified meal delivery with tracking
  • Digital record of what patient actually received
  • Result: 100% alignment between medical prescription and meal delivery

PROBLEM 5: Caregiver Time Constraints
SOLUTION:
  • Remove feeding burden through professional delivery
  • Allow caregivers to focus on emotional support
  • Reduce time spent on meal coordination
  • Less physical strain on traditional caregivers
  • Result: Better caregiver satisfaction and patient care

PROBLEM 6: Hospital Overcrowding
SOLUTION:
  • Reduce number of traditional caregivers needed
  • Professional delivery staff replace chaotic family feeding
  • Organized system reduces congestion
  • Improved patient privacy and care environment
  • Result: More organized, efficient hospital environment
